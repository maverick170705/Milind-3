Tribute to Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw

India's Legendary Military Leader and War Hero

Biography of Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw

Field Marshal Sam Hormusji Framji Jamshedji Manekshaw, also known as Sam Bahadur, was one of India's greatest military leaders. He served as the Chief of Army Staff during the 1971 Indo-Pak war, leading India to a decisive victory and the creation of Bangladesh.

“If a man says he is not afraid of dying, he is either lying or he is a Gurkha.”

Legacy

Field Marshal Manekshaw's leadership, strategic acumen, and inspiring personality continue to be remembered as a benchmark of military excellence. He was the first Indian Army officer to be promoted to the rank of Field Marshal.
